Time billing with xTimeCard

There are many wonderful open-source software for time tracking and billing out there, for instance Kimai is web based and easy to use; we wanted to learn and build a tools that integrate with our workflow rather than just issue invoice therefore we created xTimeCard.

As for any software we created these notes to start working effectively with a good understanding of the reason why functions work in the way they are actually built for ;-)

Workflow

  1. Start working with our software by creating master data of customer and project
  2. From your Gantt add meaningful tasks
  3. Review WBS
  4. Print the simplified project plan (this is what your customer understands)
  5. Record workerd hours
  6. Send to customer your activity report
  7. Bill weekly or monthly
